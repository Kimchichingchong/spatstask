Percy Jackson

Rick Riordan

Percy Jackson and the Lightning Thief follows the journey of Percy Jackson, a 12-year-old boy who discovers he is a demigod, the son of Poseidon. When Zeus's lightning bolt is stolen, Percy embarks on a quest across America to retrieve it and prevent a catastrophic war between the gods.

The Gunslinger

Stephen King

The Gunslinger is the first book in Stephen King's "The Dark Tower" series. It follows Roland Deschain, the last gunslinger, as he pursues the enigmatic Man in Black across a desolate, post-apocalyptic landscape. Along the way, Roland confronts his past and faces numerous trials, driven by his quest to reach the Dark Tower and restore order to his world.

Price:₹331

Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert.T.Kiyosaki

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki contrasts the financial philosophies of the author's two "dads": his biological father (Poor Dad), a highly educated man who struggled financially, and his best friend's father (Rich Dad), a successful entrepreneur. Through their differing approaches to money and investing, Kiyosaki illustrates key lessons on achieving financial independence and wealth.

Price:₹307
